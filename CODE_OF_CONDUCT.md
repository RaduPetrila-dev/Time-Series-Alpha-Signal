# Code of Conduct

I am committed to a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Keep feedback technical and actionable.
- Respect the time and attention of maintainers and contributors.

**Reporting**  
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact **sebastianpetrila8@gmail.com**. We will review and take appropriate action.
